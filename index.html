Do I need any finance knowledge to use this?
+
Not at all. The tool is designed for ordinary salaried professionals. You simply fill in your monthly expenses, age, and savings — the tool does all the calculations. Every number comes with a plain-language explanation.
What is the 3-bucket strategy?
+
It is a retirement withdrawal strategy where you divide your retirement corpus into 3 parts: Bucket 1 (liquid, 1–3 years of expenses), Bucket 2 (hybrid, 4–10 years), and Bucket 3 (equity, 10+ years). This ensures you always have safe money available while your long-term money keeps growing.
